    Welcome to Surrey & Hampshire Hackspace

    Welcome to Surrey & Hampshire Hackspace, a group of geeks who have founded a hackspace around the Surrey/Hampshire border. If you would like to join us, please join our Google Group, our G+ community, or pop into #sh-hackspace on freenode or via Webchat.

    We meet weekly on a Thursday evening (18:30-late) at Games Galaxy in Farnborough.
